Introduction text

My name is Malin and I'm a breathwork facilitator and vinyasa yoga teacher in Gothenburg, Sweden. In the hustling and bustling world of today we all need to find a way to de-stress and ground ourselves. For me, that means to turn to breathwork and yoga.


I remember my first encounter with yoga back in early 2000. I was reading about some movements in a health magazine that was called yoga and I tried to do them on the rug in my living room. Little did I know then what part yoga would play in my life.


On this site you'll find information on what breathwork and vinyasa, the yoga branch I teach, are. You can also book classes and sign up for my newsletter.
