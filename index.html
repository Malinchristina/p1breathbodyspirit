Certified breathwork facilitator & yoga teacher.

My name is Malin and I'm a breathwork facilitator and vinyasa yoga teacher situated in Gothenburg, Sweden. In the hustling and bustling world of today we all need need to find a way to de-stress and ground ourselves. For me, that means to turn to breathwork and yoga.


On this site you'll can find information on what breathwork and vinyasa, the yoga branch I teach, are. You can also book classes and sign up for my newsletter.
